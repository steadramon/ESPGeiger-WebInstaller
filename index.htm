  1. Select your counter type.
  2. Plug your ESP into a USB port.
  3. Click Install and pick the COM port. No device found?
  4. Wait while ESPGeiger {version} flashes.
  5. Enter your WiFi credentials when prompted, then click Visit Device.
  6. No prompt? Connect to the ESPGeiger-XXXXXX hotspot and configure WiFi there.

Note: This will erase any current firmware and settings.
